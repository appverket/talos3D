# Governance

## Stewardship

Talos3D is stewarded by Appverket LLC and is currently maintained as a
single-maintainer project.

Open source here means the code is available under a public license. It does
not mean there is a service commitment, roadmap obligation, or support
contract.

## Maintenance Stance

- Development happens when maintainer time and interest allow.
- Issues, pull requests, and feature requests may be reviewed slowly or not at
  all.
- The project may pause, slow down, or stop without notice.
- The repository may remain public even if active development stops, so others
  can continue, fork, or learn from it under the license terms.

## Decision Making

The maintainer decides what lands in the main repository.

Typical reasons to decline or close a contribution include:

- the change increases maintenance load disproportionately
- the change conflicts with Talos3D architecture or scope
- the change is valuable but not something the maintainer wants to own

Forks and separate add-ons are encouraged when interests diverge.

## Commercial Boundaries

Talos3D may be used as the basis for community, private, or commercial work as
permitted by the license.

Appverket LLC retains control of Talos3D trademarks, branding, and any
separately distributed commercial offerings.
